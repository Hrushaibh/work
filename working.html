Working of Electronic Devices

Working of Mobile Phone

A mobile phone is a wireless communication device that works using radio frequency signals.

  • Microphone converts sound into electrical signals
  • Processor acts as the brain of the device
  • Antenna transmits and receives signals
  • Battery supplies power
  • Speaker converts electrical signals into sound

Working of Television

A television receives signals through cable, satellite, or antenna and converts them into audio and video output.

  • Tuner receives the signal
  • Processor decodes audio and video
  • Display shows images
  • Speaker produces sound
