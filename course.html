Electronic Devices Course

Study of Internal Parts and Their Functions

Course Overview

This course introduces students to basic electronic devices and explains their internal components and working principles in a simple manner.

Internal Parts of Electronic Devices

Device Internal Components Function
Mobile Phone Battery, Display, Processor, Camera, Speaker, Microphone, Antenna Used for communication, internet access, photography, and multimedia.
Television Display Panel, Power Supply Unit, Motherboard, Speakers, Tuner Used to receive and display audio-visual signals.
Computer (Basic) CPU, RAM, Motherboard, Storage Device, Power Supply Used for data processing, storage, and computing tasks.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand internal parts of common electronic devices
  • Identify the function of each component
  • Learn basic electronic terminology
  • Build foundation for advanced electronics
